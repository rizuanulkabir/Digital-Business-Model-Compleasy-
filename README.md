# Compleasy — Compliance Made Easy

Compleasy is an end-to-end, AI-first compliance command center designed specifically for European small and medium-sized enterprises (the "Mittelstand"). The platform bridges the competitive gap between chaotic manual processes (spreadsheets/emails) and cost-prohibitive, oversized enterprise GRC frameworks like SAP GRC or OneTrust. 

Built to handle the strict demands of the **German Supply Chain Due Diligence Act (LkSG)** and the **European Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD)**[cite: 1], Compleasy delivers a unique market positioning: **High AI Sophistication paired with High Legal Traceability**.

---

## 🎯 The Core Problem & Solution

* **The Problem:** Mid-sized manufacturers face a complex regulatory environment. Large enterprise clients demand exhaustive risk analyses, pushing SMEs into data maintenance gaps, manual administrative overload, and significant legal liability.
* **The Solution:** Compleasy provides a lean, cloud-based platform that unifies unstructured data ingestion, automated supplier communication, compliance risk-scoring, and one-click report compilation into a zero-integration software package.

---

## 🛠️ Key Technical Modules & Architecture

The architecture is built across three operational pillars to move enterprises from compliance chaos to certification:

### 1. Discovery Automation & Deep Document Intelligence
* **RAG Technology:** Implements Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines to parse, mine, and scan unstructured PDF compliance text, supplier certificates, and ESG files instantly.
* **Automated Data Gathering:** Deploys intelligent workflows to automatically cross-reference supplier lists, detect missing documentation gaps, and handle multi-tier supplier communications.

### 2. Evidence-Based Risk Scoring
* **Text-to-Data Transformation:** Translates complex legal and qualitative language into structured quantitative indicators.
* **Legal Explanation Engine:** Leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to scan for hidden risks, assign risk scores (Low/Medium/High), and back every flag with paragraph-cited legal certainty tied to direct documentary sources.

### 3. Reporting Hub & Governance
* **Easy Report Generation™:** Automatically translates data assessments into official, audit-ready, BAFA-compliant legal reports with a single click.
* **Human-in-the-Loop Verification:** Employs an interface ensuring that AI-generated procedural actions undergo structured human validation before final export.

---

## 🔒 Security & Data Sovereignty

Given the sensitivity of corporate supply chain records, Compleasy is built strictly upon an architecture of data sovereignty:
* **DACH/German Data Residency:** Hosted completely on isolated German cloud infrastructure (e.g., Ionos, StackIT) to exclude US-based cloud infrastructure access risks.
* **Compliance Standards:** Built to align natively with GDPR, ISO 27001, and IDW PS 880 compliance architectures.
